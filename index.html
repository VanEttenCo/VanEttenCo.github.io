Metamorpho

The Element Man

Metamorpho is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. He was created in 1965 by writer Bob Haney and artist Ramona Fradon

Originally adventurer Rex Mason, he is converted into a man made of a shifting mass of chemicals after being cursed by an ancient artifact that he has retrieved.

Since the birth of my son Mason, I have decided to adopt the Element Man as my favorite superhero. You can read more about his background on Wikipedia. And if you want to view a short video on Youtube you can learn about his link with Egyptian mythology.
