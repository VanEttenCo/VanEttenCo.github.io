Working alongside founders, Northrup Street provides services to professionalize financial and accounting operations to allow businesses to scale

Setting up accounting systems, a management performance framework, or providing structure to a project can strain resources and challenge the core of a young organization. Northrup Street offers ideas, skills and time to companies who are facing these challenges. We work alongside management, serving as an advisor, coach, and project manager. Our principal is an experienced financial professional who has guided teams of various sizes.

FINANCIAL ADVISORY

For early stage companies, a performance management framework helps optimize the burn rate to allow the company to continue focusing on growth.

ACCOUNTING SERVICES

As companies scale, clear and efficient accounting processes free up precious resources as well as create credibility with potential investors.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Whether pivoting to a new opportunity or a next growth phase, project management tools ensure goals are achieved and deadlines are met.

Scott Van Etten is an international financial professional with 18+ years experience at Royal Philips, Barclays Capital, Lehman Brothers and Ernst & Young. Most recently, he worked at Royal Philips as a Program Leader driving growth for the Sonicare and Norelco brands in North America and has also held roles as Business Unit Head of Finance, Supply Chain Business Partner and FP&A Director. In addition, He has advised several early-stage companies, first as the interim CFO of Michael Andrews Bespoke in New York City and is currently an advisor for Training Systems Design, LLC, a provider of interactive learning solutions. He is a CPA, a CFA charterholder and has an MBA from IESE Business School in Barcelona, Spain.
